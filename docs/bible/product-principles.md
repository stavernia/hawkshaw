# Product Principles

- Human interaction comes first.
- Every player should have agency.
- The event transition must materially change the game.
- The mystery must remain coherent and solvable.
- The app should track what matters, not every possible detail.
- Host burden should stay low.
- Depth beats breadth for MVP.
- Information should unlock over time.
- Limited actions create tension.
- Winning should be multi-dimensional.
